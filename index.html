What it is

One wall becomes a full
shooting range.

SNYPE HOCKEY is a projection trainer. Point a projector at a wall or a shooting tarp, aim a camera at the play surface, and the room turns into a rink complete with a goalie that plays your angle.

  • Shoot from anywhere in the arc — the goalie repositions to cut the angle.
  • Green net flash on a goal, orange on a post, a HAT-TRICK banner on three in a row.
  • Runs on one Windows machine — the game, rendering and scoring all live in Unity.
  • Fully self-contained: no wearables, no pucks to charge, nothing to strap on.

Why it works

A net that plays back

Shooting at a frozen target builds a frozen shot. SNYPE HOCKEY moves, reacts and keeps score — so you train the reads and the situations, not just the reps.

  1. 🥅

    The goalie plays you

    The keeper repositions and changes pose to take away your angle. You read a live goalie and pick your spot — the game-sense a static net can never build.

  2. 🎯

    Rehearse real situations

    Choose your angle, your distance, your scenario — or let a timed drill throw new looks at you. Practice the exact shots a game actually asks for.

  3. 🎮

    Fun makes you repeat

    Mini-games and instant GOAL / SAVE feedback turn practice into play. The best drill is simply the one you want to run one more time.

  4. 🤝

    Better, together

    Every shot feeds your SNYPE score. Compare progress, chase friends and challenge teammates — development you share, instead of grind alone.

Your SNYPE score

A number for the work no one sees

SNYPE — synapse + snipe — is a score for development itself: the thousands of quiet reps behind every goal. Effort alone carries a dedicated player to Platinum; real skill — accuracy, consistency, reaction — and challenges take you toward Top 100. "What's your SNYPE?"

Two tracks run in parallel: XP-driven levels for short-term feedback, and the SNYPE score behind the rank tiers for the long mastery journey.
